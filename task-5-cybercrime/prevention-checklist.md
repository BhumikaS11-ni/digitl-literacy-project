# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

# Do’s
- Always verify the identity of the person before making any online payment.
- Check the UPI ID and name carefully before approving any transaction.
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on apps.
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store.
- Keep your mobile and banking apps updated regularly.
- Be cautious of unknown links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
- Log out from shared or public devices after use.
- Report suspicious activity immediately.

# Don’ts
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone.
- Do not accept unknown payment requests without checking.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files.
- Do not trust calls claiming to be from banks without verification.
- Never save sensitive information on public devices.
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
- Avoid responding to messages that create panic or urgency.

# UPI Safety Tips
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter your PIN to receive money.
- Always double-check before approving any "collect request".

# Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
